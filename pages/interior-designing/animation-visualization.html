Animations & Visualizations

See your virtual reality any time from anywhere.

Apart from the basic products we also prepare visualizations which additionally display the model in an appropriate form – much more accessible for presentation. The illustration becomes more realistic and is closer to the actual outlook. Architectural surveying with additional textures presented can be a basis for preparing promotion materials or occasional brochures.

We also create animations of the prepared models. The animation allows for example for presenting in a dynamic way the introduced changes in the structure, consecutive stages of an investment, or constructing or rebuilding a given object. It also enables presenting the entire process of conducting construction designs. We can as well prepare animations in the form of “virtual tour”, thanks to which the scope and the manner of carried out processing is visible together with actual size of buildings or the length of linear structures.
