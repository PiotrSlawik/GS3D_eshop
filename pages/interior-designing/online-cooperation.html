Why employ a designer?

It's simple.

In comaparison to a traditional project there is a difference in the way of communication – instead of personal meetings we can get in touch via email, Skype, telephone or instant messenger.

Thanks to this you can save time and money. The course and completion time of each stage depends on the decision making speed. For us interior designing is the biggest passion, and each and every new project is another challenge, and a source of inspiration and ideas. As designers we continuously improve our skills by searching new project possibilities. While designing interiors we use our knowledge and experience, taking into account Client’s individual expectations.

It is very important for us that the designed interior is well-thought-of, functional and to a highest extent adjusted to Customer’s needs and taste. Our projects take hours of work, and thanks to this, designed interiors are unique and will satisfy the most demanding Clients. Lightness of style and the feeling of color make our projects outstanding, timeless and original. Their distinctive feature is full elegance with a dose of craze and extravagance, yet maintaining full functionality of the interior.

More information on online project contents:

Functional project – partition walls placement, adjusting the functional arrangement of the apartment to the individual needs of the investor.

  • Two or three types of arrangement
  • Changes in partition walls placement
  • Dropped ceilings and other objects placement
  • Bathroom arrangement including ceramics
  • It is a perfect offer for those, who plan changes or think of how to arrange a developer’s apartment, and only need some hints on functional and spatial conception of the place.

Functional project will be a basis for further individual work on stylistic conception.

Complex interior arrangement project includes:

  • Demonstrative photo-realistic 3D visualizations of individual rooms
  • Planning rooms functions
  • Placing partition walls, furniture and other elements
  • Furnishing – colorful pictures
  • Sections or extensions of walls – engineering drawings with dimensions
  • Dropped ceilings and electrical outlets plan
  • Plumbing system plan
  • Floor fitting plan
  • Fixed joinery plan
  • Vertical and horizontal projections with dimensions
  • Kitchen structure with dimensions
  • Matching or individual project of furniture – including dimensions
  • Finding finishing materials and equipment (paints, furniture)
  • Luminaire, fabrics, carpets and other decoration elements)
  • Specification of surfaces – the amount of the most important finishing materials

The prices of designs are estimated individually depending on the area, type of rooms and Client’s needs
